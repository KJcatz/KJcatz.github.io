PRESERVED LEMONS AND OLIVES

4 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. fresh Spinach
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, chopped
  • 3-4 tbps. Extra Virgin Olive Oil or Argan Oil
  • ½ cup Voilet Olives, pitted and chopped
  • Peel of ½ Preserved Lemon, chopped
  • Salt and Black Pepper

DIRECTIONS

  • Wash the spinach and remove the stems only if they are thick and hard.
  • Put the leaves in a large pan, cover with a lid, and set over low heat until the leaves crumple into a soft mass. They will steam in the water that clings to them in 1-2 minutes. Drain well.
  • Heat the garlic in 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan until the aroma arises.
  • Add the chopped preserved lemon peel, the chopped olives and the cooked spinach. Season with salt and pepper, mix well, and cook over high heat for a moment or two.
  • Stir in the remaining oil, garnish with the reserved olives, and serve cold.

30 mins overall time
