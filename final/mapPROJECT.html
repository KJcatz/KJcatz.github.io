Map Design

We designed a directive map to guide a group of users follow a path with two of them holding a 5'9" sphere.

We needed a total of 4 users.

This was a group project we had to do for Information Architecture class. The designers: Karyll Catubig, Thomas Freda, Melanie Marmaras and Rachel Ladine.




Karyll - All 3D renderings of Buildings, Little Landmarks, the Path design, cover and final touches.
Thomas - All the Icon designs and Icon research. Melanie - The design of the land map (bird's eye view), research, user testing and cover.
Rachel - All the information research and idea developing and as well as user testing.
