Calendar Project

This project is to explore more about designing a cohesive series of images with a focused topic. My concept was focusing on "How to be a Better Designer." I didn't want it to be a series of actually instructing designers on what they need to do, but instead tips of what I think they should do. I used photo-manipulation on all the images made. All photos were taken from StockPholio.com.
