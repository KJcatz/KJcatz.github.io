PRACTICAL, POETIC, PERSUASIVE

We were assigned to create a series of 3 posters. Using a single word to express all three posters in different ways; Practical, Poetic, Persuasive.

The word I used was STRENGHT. This piece was for the horrific tragedy that happened here in Boston during the Marathon on April 2013. Boston Strong!
